About me


I'm a Full-Stack Developer based in Brooklyn, I enjoy creating websites and web applications as much as I like finding solutions to complex problems. Currently, I work at the New York Code and Design Academy where I continue to improve my skills while helping students through our wide-range of classes.

My hobbies are diversed as I love to try out new things, I'm a Brazilian Jiu Jitsu practicioner, a Tricker and a Freerunner enthusiast and I also do Yoga when my mood calls for it.

I play the guitar, read books and like to discover new spaces and restaurants with my friends.
